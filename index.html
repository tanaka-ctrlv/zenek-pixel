ABOUT THIS TYPEFACE

A variable pixel display face exploring playful modular forms, spacing, and motion. This typeface was designed by Tanaka Mapondera with the goal of mimmicking what it might look like when one tries to write with their non-dominant hand, which resulted in the uppercase, numbers and punctuation. The lowercase comprises of variable characters, and together with the uppercase, are inspired by the aesthetic of a 2008 computer game, Zenek Zombie by Maciej Lamberski.
